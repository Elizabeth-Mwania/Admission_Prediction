Introduction
============

Overview
--------

This project predicts graduate admissions based on several key parameters, 
helping students shortlist universities based on their profiles.

Problem Statement
-----------------

The project aims to assist students in understanding their chances of admission 
to graduate programs by analyzing factors like GRE scores, GPA, and research experience.

Dataset Information
-------------------

- **GRE Scores**: Out of 340
- **TOEFL Scores**: Out of 120
- **CGPA**: Out of 10
- **Research Experience**: Binary (0 or 1)
- **Chance of Admit**: Ranges from 0 to 1
