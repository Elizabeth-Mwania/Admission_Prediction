Data Exploration
================

This section presents the data exploration and visualization insights.

Dataset Statistics
------------------

- The dataset contains 400 rows and 9 columns.
- Higher GRE, TOEFL, CGPA, SOP, LOR scores, and research experience positively impact the chance of admission, which averages 72.4%
- Most students demonstrate strong academic profiles, with CGPAs around 8.6 and GRE scores near 317. These factors collectively explain their relatively high likelihood of admission

Visualization of relationships
------------------------------

- Strong positive correlations are visible between GRE Scores, TOEFL Scores, CGPA,
and the Chance of Admit. These variables tend to cluster and align diagonally,
indicating they are important predictors of admission chances.
